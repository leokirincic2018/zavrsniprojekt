Something about myself

Greetings,

My name is Leo Kirinčić and I am a professional designer graduated Media Design on University of North. I am specialised in branding and visual communications and have more then six years of experience in UX, Web and Mobile design. I have gained a lot of experience in handling responsible roles, through working with leading marketing agencies, startups, clusters, cultural institutions and design studios.

While working with many different clients and on interdisciplinary projects I have gained a lot of experience in a wide range of industries. This experience combined with design thinking helps me to better understand the needs of your products and costumers. My goal is to solve your problems — particularly when they concern giving customers better experience while using your products or services.
