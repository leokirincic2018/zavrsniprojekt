Monte Negro - taller & diseño

Description

Monte Negro (Black Mountain) is a workshop dedicated to the invention of design pieces, inspired by nature and its elements It seeks to convey the beauty of nature into everyday objects made of wood, metal, stone and leather.

Idea

The idea for the logo was inspired by the place where I grew up, a nature reserve in the state of Morelos Mexico known as “Sierra de Montenegro”. Target market is young workers who appreciate design and nature. Persons who wish to decorate their spaces with modern designs without losing its earthly essence.
